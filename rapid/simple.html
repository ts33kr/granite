This is a abstract base class compound that combines modal window with a data form. Basically, this component provides the skeleton that scrapes the boilerplate routine of form submission and then reacting to the response away and lets you focus on what matters to your functionality, that is setting up the layout and fields. As a basis, the service uses BoxFormular & Dialogue widgets.
